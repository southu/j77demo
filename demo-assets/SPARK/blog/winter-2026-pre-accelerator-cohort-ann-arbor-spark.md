# Meet the Winter 2026 Pre-Accelerator Cohort—and What It Could Unlock for Your Startup in Ann Arbor

Breakthroughs rarely happen alone. They happen when founders get in the same room with peers who are building at the same pace, mentors who have seen the pitfalls, and a clear plan for turning a promising product into a scalable business.

That’s the idea behind Ann Arbor SPARK’s newly announced **Winter 2026 Pre-Accelerator Cohort**, shared as part of **“Made in Michigan, Built to Scale.”** The program is built for early-stage founders who want focused support—fast—without sacrificing equity or paying fees. Read the announcement: [Made in Michigan, Built to Scale: Meet the Winter 2026 Pre-Accelerator Cohort](https://annarborusa.org/news/made-in-michigan-built-to-scale-meet-the-winter-2026-pre-accelerator-cohort/).

For founders building in **AI** and **mobility**, this cohort is also a reminder of what’s available in Ann Arbor: practical startup support, a deep talent pipeline, and an ecosystem designed to help companies move from prototype to real-world traction.

## Winter 2026 Pre-Accelerator Cohort: what SPARK announced

Ann Arbor SPARK announced the Winter 2026 Pre-Accelerator Cohort on **February 11, 2026**. Full program announcement and context are available here: [Made in Michigan, Built to Scale: Meet the Winter 2026 Pre-Accelerator Cohort](https://annarborusa.org/news/made-in-michigan-built-to-scale-meet-the-winter-2026-pre-accelerator-cohort/) and in SPARK’s broader updates via [Regional News - Ann Arbor SPARK](https://annarborusa.org/news/) (also see [Author: Ann Arbor SPARK](https://annarborusa.org/news/author/ann-arbor-spark/)).

Key program details highlighted by SPARK:

- **Five startups are supported per cohort**, which helps keep the experience high-touch and founder-focused. ([Made in Michigan, Built to Scale: Meet the Winter 2026 Pre-Accelerator Cohort](https://annarborusa.org/news/made-in-michigan-built-to-scale-meet-the-winter-2026-pre-accelerator-cohort/))
- **No fees and no equity required**, keeping participation accessible and founder-friendly. ([Made in Michigan, Built to Scale: Meet the Winter 2026 Pre-Accelerator Cohort](https://annarborusa.org/news/made-in-michigan-built-to-scale-meet-the-winter-2026-pre-accelerator-cohort/))
- The initiative traces back to **2015**, originally launched via the **gBETA** program. ([Made in Michigan, Built to Scale: Meet the Winter 2026 Pre-Accelerator Cohort](https://annarborusa.org/news/made-in-michigan-built-to-scale-meet-the-winter-2026-pre-accelerator-cohort/))

This cohort work sits within SPARK’s broader economic development mission and services: [Ann Arbor SPARK | Economic Development Services & Resources](https://annarborusa.org).

## Meet the Winter 2026 cohort startups

SPARK’s full announcement introduces the five companies selected for the Winter 2026 cohort. To see the official list and how each startup is positioned, read: [Made in Michigan, Built to Scale: Meet the Winter 2026 Pre-Accelerator Cohort](https://annarborusa.org/news/made-in-michigan-built-to-scale-meet-the-winter-2026-pre-accelerator-cohort/).

At a high level, the Winter 2026 group reflects where Ann Arbor continues to build momentum—especially in **AI** and **mobility/advanced automotive**—with founders working on technologies that benefit from customer discovery, strong partnerships, and the kind of execution discipline a short, structured program can create.

## How the Pre-Accelerator connects to the Michigan Founders Fund Pre-Accelerator

You’ll see two related names in SPARK materials:

- The **Winter 2026 Pre-Accelerator Cohort** is the cohort SPARK announced under “Made in Michigan, Built to Scale.” ([Made in Michigan, Built to Scale: Meet the Winter 2026 Pre-Accelerator Cohort](https://annarborusa.org/news/made-in-michigan-built-to-scale-meet-the-winter-2026-pre-accelerator-cohort/))
- The **Michigan Founders Fund Pre-Accelerator** is the program page that outlines funding and program components, including the grant, coaching, and mentorship model. Learn more here: [Michigan Founders Fund Pre-Accelerator](https://annarborusa.org/opportunities/michigan-founders-fund-pre-accelerator/).

In other words: the Winter 2026 cohort is the current group of participating startups, and the Michigan Founders Fund Pre-Accelerator page describes the program structure and benefits.

## Benefits for founders: $15K grant, coaching, and mentor access

The program is built to help founders make tangible progress—especially in the decisions that most often determine whether a startup gets to its next milestone.

### $15,000 non-dilutive grant

Participants receive a **$15,000 non-dilutive grant**—capital that can support customer validation, product development, early pilots, and other traction-building steps while founders keep full ownership. Details: [Michigan Founders Fund Pre-Accelerator](https://annarborusa.org/opportunities/michigan-founders-fund-pre-accelerator/).

### Individualized coaching and programming

Founders also receive **individualized coaching and programming**, designed to help clarify priorities and move faster on go-to-market decisions. Details: [Michigan Founders Fund Pre-Accelerator](https://annarborusa.org/opportunities/michigan-founders-fund-pre-accelerator/).

### Mentor support

The program pairs each founder with **dozens of mentors**, giving teams a practical way to pressure-test assumptions, avoid common missteps, and learn from operators with relevant experience. Details: [Michigan Founders Fund Pre-Accelerator](https://annarborusa.org/opportunities/michigan-founders-fund-pre-accelerator/).

For AI and mobility founders, that often includes help with:

- Turning technical capability into a clear customer value proposition
- Planning pilots and success metrics (especially in safety-critical or regulated environments)
- Building partnerships across automotive, manufacturing, and adjacent industries

## Why Ann Arbor is a strong place to build AI and mobility startups

Ann Arbor’s advantage is rarely one thing—it’s how the pieces connect.

- **Talent and research depth**: The region benefits from strong technical and entrepreneurial talent connected to the University of Michigan and the broader innovation economy.
- **A cluster effect in AI and mobility**: Ann Arbor continues to build strength in **Artificial Intelligence** and **Mobility/Advanced Automotive**, alongside biotech, clean energy, cybersecurity, IT, and R&D.
- **A support organization designed for scale**: SPARK’s role is to make it easier for companies to start, grow, and stay in the region through a mix of startup support, business development, and talent resources.

SPARK’s overview of services and the regional focus areas are available at [Ann Arbor SPARK | Economic Development Services & Resources](https://annarborusa.org), with additional cohort context in the Winter 2026 announcement: [Made in Michigan, Built to Scale: Meet the Winter 2026 Pre-Accelerator Cohort](https://annarborusa.org/news/made-in-michigan-built-to-scale-meet-the-winter-2026-pre-accelerator-cohort/).

## FAQ

### What is the Winter 2026 Pre-Accelerator Cohort?

It’s a five-startup cohort announced on February 11, 2026 under the “Made in Michigan, Built to Scale” banner, designed to support early-stage founders with structured programming and founder-friendly terms. Details: [Made in Michigan, Built to Scale: Meet the Winter 2026 Pre-Accelerator Cohort](https://annarborusa.org/news/made-in-michigan-built-to-scale-meet-the-winter-2026-pre-accelerator-cohort/).

### Does the program take equity or charge fees?

No—SPARK notes that the cohort requires **no fees** and **no equity**. ([Made in Michigan, Built to Scale: Meet the Winter 2026 Pre-Accelerator Cohort](https://annarborusa.org/news/made-in-michigan-built-to-scale-meet-the-winter-2026-pre-accelerator-cohort/))

### What funding does the Michigan Founders Fund Pre-Accelerator provide?

The program includes a **$15,000 non-dilutive grant**. Details: [Michigan Founders Fund Pre-Accelerator](https://annarborusa.org/opportunities/michigan-founders-fund-pre-accelerator/).

### What support do founders receive beyond funding?

The program includes **individualized coaching and programming** and access to **dozens of mentors** for each founder. Details: [Michigan Founders Fund Pre-Accelerator](https://annarborusa.org/opportunities/michigan-founders-fund-pre-accelerator/).

### Where can I find the list of the five Winter 2026 cohort startups?

SPARK lists the selected companies in the official announcement: [Made in Michigan, Built to Scale: Meet the Winter 2026 Pre-Accelerator Cohort](https://annarborusa.org/news/made-in-michigan-built-to-scale-meet-the-winter-2026-pre-accelerator-cohort/).

### How does SPARK support startups beyond the pre-accelerator?

SPARK offers a broader set of economic development and startup resources—such as Entrepreneur Boot Camp, coworking, startup acceleration support, coaching and mentorship, and connections to funding pathways like SPARK Capital. Learn more: [Ann Arbor SPARK | Economic Development Services & Resources](https://annarborusa.org).

Ann Arbor is increasingly recognized for strengths in **AI** and **Mobility/Advanced Automotive**, supported by a strong talent pipeline and an ecosystem focused on helping companies start and scale.
