# Getting started with revenue intelligence (Part 1)

Revenue intelligence brings together data from calls, emails, and meetings so teams can see what’s working and what isn’t.

In this first part we cover the basics: what it is, who it’s for, and how to get value quickly.

## What is revenue intelligence?

Revenue intelligence is the practice of using data and AI to understand and improve revenue-related activities. It typically includes:

- Conversation capture and analysis
- Deal and pipeline insights
- Coaching and enablement

## Who it’s for

Customer-facing teams—sales, success, and support—benefit most when the whole organization aligns on a single source of truth.
