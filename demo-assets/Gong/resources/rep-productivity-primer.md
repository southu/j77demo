# Rep productivity primer

Reps spend too much time on manual data entry and hunting for context. Here’s how to change that.

## Automate the busywork

Capture calls and meetings automatically. Push insights into the CRM so reps don’t have to.

## Give reps the context they need

Surfacing the right information at the right time—before a call or in the moment—helps reps prepare and follow up effectively.
